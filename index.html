Defination

The Internet is the biggest world-wide communication network of computers. The Internet has millions of smaller domestic, academic, business,and government networks, which together carry many different kinds of information. The short form of internet is the 'net'. It is used by billions of people all over the world.The Internet is a global computer network for communication actually nothing more than a basic computer network. The Internet simply moves data from one place to another, so that we can chat, browse and share,

with packet switching, many people can use the Internet at the same time.January 1, 1983 is considered the official birthday of the Internet.Prior to this, the various computer networks did not have a standard way to communicate with each other. A new communications protocol was established called Transfer Control Protocol/Internetwork Protocol (TCP/IP).
